Dark Souls III Game Review

Image Source: Steam

By Jeffrey Choi | May 6, 2026

Dark Souls 3 adds onto a popular video game series that is considered dark fantasy and a difficult RPG game style. It was released in 2016 by FromSoftware Inc. and greatly improves on gameplay mechanics and visual graphics in comparison to the previous games. I have replayed the game many times to this day, but my first time was a struggle to explore the world and defeat the bosses. Even with that being the case, I have come to love this game and the series as a whole because of the intense combat and rewarding feeling of beating bosses after struggling for so long. The game also features customization to weapons, armors, and spells. These make the game both personal and add to the challenge that requires you to become better at the game's combat. It is one my personal favorite games of all time, and new games by both the same developers and new ones in recent years have expanded on the genre with modern game development. Many people might quit due to the challenge, but completing the game is an accomplishment.
